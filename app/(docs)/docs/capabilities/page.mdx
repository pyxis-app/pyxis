# What Pyxis can & can't do

Honest framing is a product principle here, not a disclaimer. Here's the line.

## What Pyxis does well

- **Multi-source synthesis** no single dashboard gives you in one place.
- **Real-time freshness** with a per-source timestamp on every figure.
- **Honest gaps** — unavailable data is disclosed, never hallucinated.
- **Pre-listing coverage** for new tokens via DexScreener.

## What Pyxis is not

<HonestGap title="Pyxis does not claim to be">
- Institutional-grade research or full due diligence.
- An investment recommendation — no "buy", no "sell", no price targets.
- A vesting/unlock analyzer, a whale tracker, or a smart-money flow tool.
- A source of team or VC background checks.
- A predictor of time-bounded outcomes.
</HonestGap>

Think of Pyxis as one step above a Twitter recap and several steps below a
paid institutional report: a fast, honest starting point for your own research.
