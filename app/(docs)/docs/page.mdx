# What is Pyxis?

Pyxis is a Web3 intelligence swarm. Five autonomous agents research any token,
chain, protocol, or narrative in parallel, then merge their findings into a
single, source-stamped briefing.

<Callout>
Pyxis is **a fast, honest starting point — not a substitute for due diligence.**
Every number is stamped with its source and timestamp, and gaps are disclosed
rather than guessed.
</Callout>

## The swarm at a glance

A research run is five sequential LLM calls. Each agent has one job and reads its
own bundle of live data:

<AgentGrid>
  <AgentCard agent="commander">Reads your topic, classifies it, and writes focused queries for the other agents.</AgentCard>
  <AgentCard agent="scout">Information & narrative — news, announcements, contract-verification flags.</AgentCard>
  <AgentCard agent="analyst">Quantitative readout — price, TVL, liquidity, supply, yields, holder concentration.</AgentCard>
  <AgentCard agent="sentinel">Social pulse & sentiment — Twitter, Reddit, governance, macro overlay.</AgentCard>
  <AgentCard agent="synthesizer" full>Merges the three findings into one briefing with sections adapted to the topic, then appends a Data Freshness table.</AgentCard>
</AgentGrid>

## Source-stamped, always

Every data point carries a freshness marker — `[live]` or `[cached]` — and the
briefing ends with a table listing each source, the endpoint, and when it was
sampled. You always know where a number came from.

<HonestGap>
Not institutional-grade research. No buy/sell recommendations, no price
predictions, no whale-tracking or vesting analysis. When a source is
unavailable, Pyxis says so — it never fabricates a number.
</HonestGap>
