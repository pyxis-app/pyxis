# FAQ

## Is Pyxis free?

Yes — free during beta. Original pricing appears struck through in the app and
nothing is charged today.

## Why do I have to connect a wallet?

Pyxis uses a wallet signature (SIWE) to authenticate you and keep your research
history private. Signing is free and creates no transaction.

## Which chains are supported?

Contract verification covers EVM chains: Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Polygon,
Optimism, and BSC. On Solana, Pyxis reports on-chain metadata and holder
distribution rather than source-code verification.

## Can I share a briefing?

Yes. Every briefing has a public, read-only link that exposes the research but
never your wallet address.

## How current is the data?

Most figures are fetched live; some are briefly cached. The Data Freshness table
at the end of each briefing shows exactly what was sampled and when.

## Is this financial advice?

No. Pyxis is a research starting point, not an investment recommendation. Always
do your own due diligence.
